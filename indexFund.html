Index Fund Investing

Designed for your dream retirement

Our index fund option allows you to create a personalized index. It takes the S&P500 companies, but allows you to pick a certain market cap range and how much money to invest. This allows for plenty of diversification for risk-averse investors, but with easy personalization. We would also recommend this to users with a low starting balance.
